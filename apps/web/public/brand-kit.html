Logo Usage

Do

  • Keep clear space ≥ the height of the mark on all sides.
  • Use the reversed (white) logo on dark or photo backgrounds.
  • Use the mark alone for favicons, app icons and avatars.
  • Maintain the red-to-dark-red gradient on the badge.

Don’t

  • Don’t recolour the wordmark or swap Rich/Deals colours.
  • Don’t place the primary logo on a busy or red background.
  • Don’t stretch, rotate or add shadows/outlines to the logo.
  • Don’t rebuild the mark with a different discount glyph.
